    Revamp

  • Placement Cell & Internship Cell

    We propose a Placement Cell that runs yearlong, ensuring more student representation and an associated Internship Cell. Moreover Technical training classes which will prove useful for placements shall be implemented.

  • Set Her Free

    We will strive to bring an end to the double standards imposed on CET women. We propose to extend the LH curfew to 9pm after ensuring all the security measures required for the same.

  • CET Live

    An Online Forum to broadcast all the relevant notifications related to the College/University.

  • E-Library

    To uphold the idea of availability of quality books for all, we propose to build a Platform to Buy and Sell Secondhand Books and Notes- Without Commision.

  • Food for all, Shelter for all

    The college union proposes to take over all the vacated IISER hostels and accommodate all the students residing outside the Mens Hostel and provide Mess facility for all.

  • Stronger Together

    Special remedial classes in English and a staff council member to take care of the affairs of our friends from the North-East.

    • Revive

  • Night Life @ CET

    A Cafeteria, Chilling Spots, and Library facilities that run late into the night to give life to the nights at CET.

  • NET for all

    Centralised WiFi facility in college to uphold the idea of free internet facility for all.

  • Team De’ Expertise

    Micro-project guidance for KTU students with the help of M-Tech,MCA and MBA students, and Project funding through eminent companies and Alumni.

  • Hello Junior

    A Mentorship Programme for freshers, to help them settle into the new world of college life and take calculated decisions.

  • Technical Secretary

    A Technical Secretary post to oversee and bring together all the Technical Events and Clubs.

  • Career Guidance

    Orientation classes by accomplished Career Experts, to help the students tackle competitive exams like CAT, GRE, GMAT, GATE, IAS, XAT.

  • KTU Minor Courses

    The new initiative introduces an opportunity for KTU Students to take minor courses during the 4 years of Btech. This simply means students will be able to take minor courses parallely with their Btech course. The minor course can be on fine arts like music, art , or even an LLB.

    • Rejoice

  • Theatre of Dreams

    Facility for Big Screen Projection of CINEMA, LIVE SPORTS and other events inside the college.

  • DHWANI : The way it’s meant to be

    We promise to conduct Dhwani ’17 in all its splendor and glory, and regain the trust and reputation associated with CET and every CETian.

  • “Ormacheppu”

    A YearBook to immortalize in its pages, the emotions and memories of every Final Year CETian.

  • Convocation Night

    A Convocation Night to make Graduation a grand celebration.

  • MBA Involement

    We look forward to utilizing the immense potential vested in our MBA batches. We promise a minimum of at least one representative from the MBA batch in every organizing committee. p>

  • Youth Fest

    Practice sessions and training programs for the students to equip them to face the competition, and lift CET up the ranks in the University Youth Festival.

  • Yuva, Disha, Drishti

    We will strive to revive “Drishti” in all its original glory and conduct “Disha” for the 1st years and “Yuva” for the second year students who were deprived of the chance to enjoy Disha.

  • PG Fest

    We shall see a revamped PG fest that will transcend into levels never seen before.It will be the revival of an experience that all PG students have dreamed of.
